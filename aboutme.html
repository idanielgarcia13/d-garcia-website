About me

Hello! My name is Daniel.

I am currently a full-time* student at Waubonsee Community College and College of DuPage. My education plan is to earn a Bachelors degree in Computer Science and Computer Information Systems from Lewis University.

Some of my interest are writing code, designing UI, drones, dark mode, Apple, Tesla, AI/ML, news, and the environment.
