Design Tools

Sketch

Sketch is a powerful design tool that I use for responsive web and mobile. Before I knew about Sketch, I would make my UI designs in apps like Keynote and Pages. What I loved about Sketch is that it was easy to transfer from Apple's iWork apps. It was exciting to see an app be more dedicated to design. Features like prototyping and automatic resizing allowed me to do even more with my designs.

It's interesting to see new design apps like Figma which is more cloud-based and on the collaborative-side.
